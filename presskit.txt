ABOUT THE GAME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
OLLIM is a 2D side-scrolling Metroidvania game developed as a GAM250 class
project at DigiPen Institute of Technology. The player takes on the role of a
boy who once guarded the stars. Upon receiving a divine command to save the
twelve zodiac constellations that have fallen to the earth, he sets off on an
adventure across the surface world.

The game features a meticulously designed Metroidvania progression: defeating
each boss grants new abilities that unlock exploration of the next area. Players
wield a unique starlight-reflection mechanic, fighting fluidly without ever
attacking directly, using reflected light to redeem each fallen constellation
and restore it to the sky.


HOW TO PLAY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Left-click to reflect starlight back at enemies. Observe and dodge incoming
starlight attacks while staying aware of dangerous platforms beneath your feet.
Each boss fight demands careful observation, precise timing, and mastery of the
reflection mechanic to prevail.

Controls:
  - Movement:    A (left), D (right)
  - Jump:        W or Space
  - Dash:        Left Shift
  - Interact:    F (read signboards, activate campfires, etc.)
  - Reflect:     Left Mouse Click (reflect starlight with shield)
  - Shield:      Hold Right Mouse Click (create shield at cursor position)


BOSS SPOTLIGHT — TAURUS, THE BULL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Once a radiant constellation adorning the night sky, Taurus lost most of its
starlight to a mysterious, unknown force and plummeted to the earth. Consumed
by confusion and pain, it now lashes out at anything that draws near, unleashing
volleys of residual starlight at the player.

To redeem Taurus, the player must reflect its own starlight back, gradually
restoring the brilliance it has lost and guiding this fallen constellation back
to the heavens.

THIS IS NOT YOUR TYPICAL ACTION GAME
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In OLLIM, you never attack. Instead, you reflect starlight — turning every
enemy's power against them in a combat system unlike anything you have played
before. Built from the ground up on a custom OpenGL engine, OLLIM fuses
Metroidvania exploration with intense boss rush encounters where survival
depends on observation, timing, and reflexes rather than brute force. If you
are looking for a fresh take on 2D action that challenges the conventions of
the genre, OLLIM is the story worth telling.
